Marker Icons

Here is a collection of marker icons that include numbers 1 to 99. The collection includes the following kinds of icons:

  • Large teardrop icons in four colors: blue, red, green, and yellow.
  • Large teardrop icons in two colors: blue and red, and green and red.
  • Small square icons in four colors: blue, red, green, and yellow.
  • Small square marker icons in two colors: blue and red, and green and red.
  • A blank icon of each kind of marker listed above.

The icons were prepared using ImageMagick 6.2.5. The initial colors for the icons were obtained from Sam Kuhn's web hues. The teardrop icons are compatible with the Google teardrop icons. The square icons are compatible with the Yahoo! square icons. By default, Mash-o-matic uses the icons in this collection.
